~~ Future Realtors Limited ~~

This website is based around the idea of a realtor in the future advertising and selling properties or apartments.
This website contains a main page, a contacts page, and a page of the homes they are selling.


~ Index (Main) Page ~

This page will act as an introduction to the company and give some examples of what customers can expect for their range of
houses to look like. 

~ Contact Page ~

This page shows the contact information of the company and where they can be reached if needed.

~ Homes Page ~

This page displays some of the homes available for rent or purchase.
